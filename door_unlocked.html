CABIN DOOR Annunciator

Airstair or Cargo Door Unlocked Warning

Warning Description
Annunciator
CABIN DOOR
Condition
Airstair or cargo door is unlatched or improperly secured
Trigger Point
Door position sensor detects unlatched condition
CRITICAL WARNING
WARNING: Do not attempt to check the security of the airstair or cargo door in flight. Remain as far from the door as possible with seatbelts securely fastened.

Cabin Pressure Reduction Methods
Descending

Initiate descent to lower altitude where cabin pressure differential is naturally reduced.

Cabin Altitude Setting

Adjust pressurization controller to select higher cabin altitude setting, reducing differential pressure.

CABIN PRESS Switch

Use CABIN PRESS switch in DUMP position to rapidly reduce cabin pressure (emergency use only).

Safety Considerations
Structural Integrity

An unlatched door in flight poses significant risk of sudden depressurization and potential structural damage if the door opens or detaches.

Passenger Safety

All occupants must remain seated with seatbelts securely fastened. No one should approach the affected door area during flight.

Emergency Landing

Land at the nearest suitable airport. Consider runway length, emergency services availability, and weather conditions when selecting landing site.

Post-Landing Procedures
Inspection

After landing and securing aircraft, conduct thorough inspection of door mechanism, latches, and seals.

Maintenance

Do not resume flight until door system has been inspected and certified airworthy by qualified maintenance personnel.

Documentation

Document the event in aircraft logbook and complete required safety reports per company procedures.
